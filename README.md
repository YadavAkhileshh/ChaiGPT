# ChaiGPT - AI Twitter Bot

An intelligent Twitter bot that creates viral tech tweets by combining real-time news with AI-powered humor. Generates witty Hinglish content that resonates with the tech community.

## What It Does

- **Smart Content Creation**: Analyzes trending tech news from Hacker News and Google RSS feeds
- **AI-Powered Writing**: Uses Google Gemini to craft engaging tweets with perfect mix of Hindi and English
- **Viral Tweet Formula**: Creates controversial yet fun takes on tech trends that spark discussions
- **Auto Engagement**: Posts main tweets with thoughtful replies to boost interaction
- **24/7 Operation**: Runs continuously, posting fresh content every 4 hours
- **Fallback System**: Always posts something interesting even if APIs fail

## How It Works

1. **Data Collection**: Scrapes Hacker News top stories and Google tech RSS
2. **AI Processing**: Gemini analyzes trends and creates witty commentary
3. **Content Generation**: Produces viral-worthy tweets with Hindi/English mix
4. **Auto Posting**: Publishes to Twitter with engaging replies
5. **Continuous Loop**: Repeats every 4 hours automatically
